Autosave every seconds
Enabling this will completely flood your browser history (one entry for every autosave), however if consider that to be worth the peace of mind of never losing your progress unless something very unexpected happens, go ahead!

Also helps keep history slightly cleaner (the video may also pause randomly (eg. other extensions and buffering) before it starts and this helps to not log that)
